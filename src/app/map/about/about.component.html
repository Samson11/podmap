About

Podmap is an open-source app whose goals are:

  • to map the world’s podcasts
  • to help you discover podcasts produced near you
  • to help you discover where your favorite podcasts are created

Add the location of your podcast or help us out by submitting a location for any podcast that doesn’t have one yet. Just start with a podcast search. Location submissions must be moderated before they appear on the map.

The granularity of location is confined to cities, so where you may want to enter "Brooklyn, NY" as a location, you’ll have to enter New York. Though this is slightly less accurate, it allows for better aggregation.
